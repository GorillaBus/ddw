Welcome to Planetario demo!

This simulation uses gravity force computed between different moving bodies of different mass. The system has stable orbits and sub-orbits that can be generated in many levels of hierarchy.

Controls

  • Press Esc key to pause/resume the simulation
  • Use the mouse wheel or +/- keys to zoom in/out
  • (Zoom in to discover sub-orbits, zoom out to discover distant bodies)
  • Use keys 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 to move (and attach) the camera to the different planets
  • (You can observe the system from the perspective of a moving object by moving the camera to a different object)
  • Use the arrow keys to freely move around without changing the view perspective.
  • Use the Q and E keys to rotate the camera left/right.
